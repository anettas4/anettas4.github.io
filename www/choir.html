    Choir

    I was the conductor for the choir in my music sorority, Sigma Alpha Iota. This treble choir meets only once a week and performs at the end of the quarter in a recital we put on. Experience singing ranges from never having sung in a formal choir before to semi-professional vocal majors. Repertoire is comprised of 100% female composers and is chosen to allow every individual to achieve a sense of success and pride in the final product and the rehearsal process.

    Although primarily a “band person” I have participated in choir since I was in second grade and enjoy singing whether it is in settings like choirs or in informal settings. I believe that everyone can sing. Similar to my ideas about band, I believe that a music education can be achieved utilizing popular music and that it gets students interested in singing, but other pieces can be incorporated as well.
