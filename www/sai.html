    Sigma Alpha Iota

    Love and Roses

    Conductor

    I served as conductor for our chapter of SAI for a year. After taking a choral conducting class, I felt the need to move away from the ensemble and to the podium, helping every individual find their voice and revive their love of music or develop a love of choir. I wanted my music selections to represent the people I saw in front of me, so I programmed all female composers. Our choir reached new heights and our repertoire was accessible while still providing challenges for those that needed it.

    Crush Party Chair

    One of the other big projects I undertook during my time at SAI was being crush party chair. I organized a crush party- a casual event for the chapter. In the midst of basketball season, the biggest battle was finding a time most people could make it. I contacted many local venues and ultimately chose a trampoline park as the location. I was in charge of handling all the financials as well as organizing rides and attendence. Overall, it was a huge success!

    Philanthropy Chair

    The philanthropy chair is responsible for picking an organization or cause to raise money for or otherwise assist. Since Relay for Life was upon us, I decided to do something to raise money for Relay for Life. In years past, they had done a karaoke night which raised $20. I decided to instead do a bake sale and we raised over $300 dollars for Relay for Life!
