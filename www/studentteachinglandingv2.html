    What did I learn?

    Instrument Repair

    In all my placements, I had to improvise quick repairs that would let the student play for the class period and hopefully longer. Since many students were lower income, we did our best to repair instruments without having them go to the shop.

    IEPs and Special Needs

    Throughout my time student teaching, I worked with students of varying abilities. I studied their IEPs to make sure I was adequately meeting all their accomodations and setting them up for success.

    Technology

    Technology was a large part of my middle school and high school placements. By using technology, I was able to show students what I was hearing as well as give them information about the music they needed to succeed.
