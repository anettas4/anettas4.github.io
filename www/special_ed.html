    Special Education

    Coursework and Work Experience

    Coursework

    As part of my coursework, I spent time at a self-contained school in their music classrooms. I learned ways to incorporate music into a special education classroom. I learned a lot about adaptive instruments and other technology available to individuals with special needs to allow them to participate more fully in the music-making experience. It was an experience that gave me confidence in working in a self-contained classroom.

    Work Experience

    I spent two summers with the Maine-Niles Association of Special Recreation working as a camp counselor and an inclusion aid. I worked with individuals of all ages and all ability levels. As a camp-counselor in a self-contained camp, I worked with not only my camper, but their interactions with all of the campers and stepped in wherever needed. As an inclusion aid, I worked with one student and made sure he was included and participated in the camp to the fullest. This experience has given me the skills and strategies needed to include students of all abilities into my classroom.
